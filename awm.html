Product Design: AWM Sniper Rifle

AWM Sniper Rifle: Photorealistic Rendering

Inspired by Playerunknowns Battlegrounds, this fun project was an excercise for me to design and render the weapon in the SolidWorks environment. Even the rendering was done using the SW native render tool PhotoView 360. I entertained some 'decaling' during the render process to mimic the sticker texture on the body.

Design Tool: SolidWorks
Render Tool: SolidWorks PhotoView 360
