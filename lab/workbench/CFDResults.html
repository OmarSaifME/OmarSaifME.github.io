Workbench: CFD Results

Result Discussion & CFD-Post

Result Discussion: Built as an extension of the CFD Manual for a 2D airfoil simulation, the CFD Results page aims to educate students and enthusiasts to dig deep into Computational Fluid Dynamics, and what different results represent in the context of their 2D CFD simulation.

The CFD simulation was done for a NACA0012 (Chord Length = 1m) airfoil, with a 2° Angle of Attack and a Flow Speed of 45 m/s (approximate).

Additional results will be shared here soon.
