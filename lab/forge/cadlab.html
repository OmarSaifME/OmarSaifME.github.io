Designs! Designs Everywhere!

Welcome to CAD Lab - a collection of my CAD designs I have made as a professional (and as a hobbyist) design engineer. This collection ranges from 'boring' industrial machineries to entertaining prototypes of drones. Apologies for not taking the time to render them properly, but they are all just one Keyshot project away from looking like advertisements.

The models are divided into two groups. In one, I showcase various industrial products I've designed as an engineer (industry context); while in the other, a few concept (and fun side projects - no one paid for any of those!) products.

Don't steal my designs!
