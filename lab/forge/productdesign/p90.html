Product Design: P90 PDW

P90 PDW: Photorealistic Rendering

In my earlier days of 3D design, this was the project that taught me how to handle cpu-heavy tasks like rendering (in PhotoView 360, GPU rendering was not an option back then) that requires ray tracing. Additionally, I explored text extrusion feature in SolidWorks (just in case it wasn't obvious from the pictures!). Inspired by Call of Duty 4, this project helped me build my confidence back in the days of designing 3D objects with non-conventional geometries.

Design Tool: SolidWorks
Render Tool: SolidWorks PhotoView 360
