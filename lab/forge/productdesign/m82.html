Product Design: Barrette M82

Barrette M82 SR: Photorealistic Rendering

After posting another design on facebook, one of my cousin dared me (for a coffee) to design a popular sniper rifle from the Call of Duty franchise. To his surprise, I designed and rendered this within a day and tagged him in the post. Awestrucked, he did buy me a coffee. Although I promised him I'd teach him how to design stuff in SolidWorks!

Design Tool: SolidWorks
Render Tool: SolidWorks PhotoView 360
